🥛 Our Flagship Product

Fresh Buffalo Milk

Rich, creamy, and brimming with natural nutrition. Our buffalo milk is milked fresh every morning and delivered to your doorstep before 7 AM. No added water, no preservatives — just the pure, full-cream goodness that your family deserves.

Why buffalo milk? It's naturally higher in calcium, protein, and healthy fats compared to cow milk. It's the preferred choice for growing children, athletes, and anyone who values genuine nutrition.

✨ Traditional Bilona Method

Pure Desi Ghee

Made the traditional bilona way — our ghee starts with hand-churned butter from cultured buffalo milk curd, then slow-cooked to perfection. The result? Golden, granular ghee with an aroma that fills your entire kitchen.

Our ghee is rich in vitamins A, D, E, and K. It's the secret ingredient behind great Indian cooking — from fluffy rotis to aromatic dal tadka.

🥣 Naturally Set

Fresh Dahi (Curd)

Thick, creamy, and naturally set from 100% buffalo milk. Our dahi is made using traditional culture (jaman) passed down through generations — no thickeners, no gelatin, no artificial anything.

Enjoy it plain, use it to make chaas (buttermilk), raita, or lassi. Buffalo milk dahi is naturally richer and creamier than cow milk curd — perfect for your daily meals and digestive health.
