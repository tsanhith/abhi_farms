Our Story

A Family Legacy of Pure Dairy

What started as a small family dairy in our village has grown into a trusted name for pure, fresh buffalo milk. Our grandfather began this journey with just a few buffaloes and a simple belief: good milk comes from happy, healthy animals.

"We wake up at 4 AM so your milk is at your door by 7 AM. That's not a tagline — that's our life."

Today, we continue that tradition with the same values — no shortcuts, no compromises, just pure milk the way nature intended.
