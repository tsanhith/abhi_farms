Got Questions?

We've Got Answers

Here are the most common questions we receive from customers. Can't find your answer? Contact us directly!

We provide fresh, unprocessed buffalo milk. It is not ultra-pasteurized like packaged milk brands. We recommend boiling the milk once at home before consumption, which is the traditional and safest way to enjoy fresh milk. This preserves the natural taste, nutrition, and enzymes that are lost in industrial processing.
Our buffalo milk naturally contains 7-8% fat, which is significantly richer than cow milk (3-4%) or most packaged milk brands. This is full-cream milk — we do not remove or adjust the fat content. It's the reason our milk tastes so rich, creamy, and satisfying.
Absolutely not. We have a zero-tolerance policy on adulteration. Our milk goes straight from the buffalo to the packaging — no water, no preservatives, no powder reconstitution. Every batch is tested for purity before delivery. We stake our family's reputation on this promise.
We currently deliver across multiple areas in and around the city. You can check if we deliver to your area by entering your pin code on our homepage delivery checker. We're constantly expanding — if we don't cover your area yet, reach out and we'll let you know when we do!
Morning deliveries are made between 5:30 AM and 7:30 AM so your fresh milk is ready before breakfast. We operate Monday through Saturday. For special timing requests, please contact us directly on WhatsApp.
Yes! We offer flexible subscription plans — daily, alternate day, or weekly delivery. You can subscribe for milk, paneer, dahi, or any combination. Simply message us on WhatsApp with your preferred schedule, quantity, and address, and we'll set it up for you. You can pause or cancel anytime.
Yes, absolutely! We welcome farm visits and encourage our customers to see where their milk comes from. You can schedule a visit by contacting us on WhatsApp or through our contact page. We'll show you around the farm, introduce you to our buffaloes, and walk you through our milking and quality processes.
Yes — we supply buffalo milk and dairy products in bulk to sweet shops, restaurants, caterers, hotels, and retailers. We offer competitive wholesale pricing with reliable daily delivery. For bulk inquiries, please reach out to us directly and we'll discuss pricing, quantities, and delivery schedules.
For household deliveries, the minimum order is 1 liter of milk per delivery. For paneer, ghee, and dahi, minimums vary by product. There's no minimum for one-time orders — you can start with any quantity and increase as you like. For bulk/wholesale, please contact us for details.
We accept payments via UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and cash on delivery. For subscription customers, we typically settle payments on a weekly or monthly basis for convenience. All payment details will be shared after your first order confirmation.
