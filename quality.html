Our Standards

Your Trust Is Our Highest Standard

We believe quality isn't just about the product — it's about every step that leads to it. From the fodder our buffaloes eat to the moment milk reaches your doorstep, every stage is carefully controlled.

🏅

FSSAI Certified

Our dairy operations are fully licensed under the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India. We comply with all food safety regulations to ensure our products meet national quality benchmarks.

🧪

Daily Quality Testing

Every batch of milk undergoes fat content, SNF (Solids-Not-Fat), and adulteration tests before packaging. We reject any batch that doesn't meet our internal standards — which are stricter than regulatory minimums.

🚿

Hygienic Milking Process

Our milking area is washed and sanitized twice daily. Milking is done with clean, dedicated equipment and our staff follows strict hand-hygiene protocols before every session.

🌿

Natural Feed Only

Our buffaloes are fed fresh green fodder, dry hay, and mineral supplements — never silage or chemical growth boosters. Healthy feed creates healthy milk. It's that simple.

🩺

Regular Veterinary Care

A qualified veterinarian visits our farm regularly for health checkups, vaccinations, and preventive care. Healthy animals are the foundation of safe, nutritious dairy products.

❄️

Cold Chain Maintained

After milking, our products are stored at recommended temperatures and delivered in insulated containers. We maintain a strict cold chain to preserve freshness from farm to your door.
